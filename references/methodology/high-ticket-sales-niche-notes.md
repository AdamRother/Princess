# Sales coaching niche — reference notes

Used by Phase 1.5 (competitor curation) to build the niche reference vector for `niche_similarity` scoring. Also used as context when generating scripts.

---

## Niche definition

**Channel owner:** Female sales coach. Content covers the full sales spectrum — the channel is not limited to high-ticket, but high-ticket and sales-call closing are core strengths.

**In scope:** Sales training, coaching, and education for anyone who sells — including:
- Remote high-ticket closers and appointment setters
- Founders selling their own offers on calls ($3k–$100k+)
- Sales reps for coaches, consultants, agencies, info-product creators
- SDRs and BDRs doing outbound prospecting
- General salespeople wanting to improve their close rate, call skills, mindset, or scripts
- Anyone learning sales as a career or skill

**Out of scope (score these channels LOW in niche_similarity):**
- Real estate sales (specialized license track, different audience)
- Car sales or retail sales (floor-based, not call-based)
- Generic motivational content that only mentions "sales" in passing
- Door-to-door or field sales (different skill set)
- Recruiting / talent acquisition framed as sales
- Pure SaaS product demo / enterprise account management content

**The litmus test:** Does this channel's content teach someone how to sell better — on a call, in an outreach message, or in a coaching/consulting context? If yes, in scope.

## Topic territories

Breakout videos in this niche cluster into these territories. Use these when evaluating niche fit and generating topic ideas:

### Closing frameworks and call structure
- 1-call close vs. 2-call close strategy
- Discovery call frameworks (what to ask, in what order)
- Tonality and pacing on sales calls
- How to handle "let me think about it"
- How to handle "I need to talk to my spouse/partner"
- Price objections: "it's too expensive", "I can't afford it"
- The soft close vs. the assumptive close
- How to follow up without being pushy

### Objection handling (broader)
- The objection behind the objection
- Reframing price vs. value
- Handling "I'm not ready"
- Handling ghosting after a positive call

### Prospecting & outreach
- Cold calling scripts that book meetings
- Cold DM / cold email opening lines
- How to qualify leads before the call
- Building a personal outreach system
- LinkedIn outreach for coaches and consultants

### Identity and mindset
- Getting over fear of rejection / call reluctance
- How to stay detached from outcomes while still caring
- Abundance vs. scarcity mindset in sales
- Confidence on the phone
- Sales identity (who you are, not just what you do)

### Career path and income
- How to break into sales / get a sales job
- Setter-to-closer progression
- How much salespeople actually make (commission structures, OTE)
- Remote sales jobs: how to find and vet them
- Full-time vs. freelance closing

### Scripts and frameworks
- Word-for-word opening lines
- Full call scripts (discovery, closing, follow-up)
- The exact question sequence that uncovers pain
- How to present price without flinching

### Coaching and training methodology
- How to review your own call recordings
- Roleplay and practice techniques
- How to track your own metrics (close rate, show rate)
- Getting a sales mentor or coach
