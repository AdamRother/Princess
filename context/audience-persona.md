# Audience Persona — Behind the Sale (@BehindtheSale)

## Who she's talking to
Sales reps and high-ticket closers who are stuck below their income ceiling. Primarily:
- Remote closers and appointment setters working on commission for coaches, consultants, or agencies
- People who have some sales experience but can't break through to consistent $10k–$20k months
- Reps who know the tactics but are losing deals they feel they should be closing
- Anyone who has been trained on "objection handling" and feels like it's not working

## Where they are right now
- Making somewhere between $2,800 and $8,000/month inconsistently
- Getting hit with "let me think about it" and "I need to talk to my partner" on every call
- Following scripts and word tracks — and feeling fake doing it
- Anxious before calls, uncertain about their approach
- Wondering what the top 1% are doing differently
- They've watched the YouTube videos, bought the courses, followed the frameworks — and still not closing consistently

## What they actually want
- To hit $10k–$20k months consistently, not just occasionally
- To feel confident and convicted on every call, not nervous
- To close without feeling pushy or manipulative
- To understand WHY deals fall through, not just get another script
- "Life-changing income" — not just a raise, a new life

## What they believe (the wrong beliefs she's correcting)
- "I need better objection handling scripts" (she teaches: eliminate objections, not handle them)
- "Being direct is rude or pushy" (she teaches: directness is respect and service)
- "$10,000 is a lot of money" (she calls this a deal-killing belief)
- "I need to be liked to close" (she: the ones who get liked get ghosted)
- "I just need to find the right word track" (she: word tracks without conviction don't close)
- "The close is the most important part of the call" (she: if you get to objections, you've already lost)

## Their gender and background
Mixed — men and women. Younger-skewing (25–40). Many are from non-traditional backgrounds who got into sales as a path to financial freedom (not corporate sales reps). Her content resonates strongly with women in sales but her audience is fully co-ed.

## The transformation she sells
From inconsistent, script-dependent, "nice" closer who gets ghosted → to conviction-led, standard-setting closer who gets a yes before they even pitch. From $5K months to $20K+ months.
